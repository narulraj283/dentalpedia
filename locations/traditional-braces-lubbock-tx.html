About Traditional Braces in Lubbock

Traditional metal braces use brackets and wires to gradually straighten teeth and correct bite issues. They remain the most effective option for severe misalignment and are suitable for all ages.

If you live in Lubbock, Texas or the surrounding South area, finding the right dental provider for traditional braces is an important decision. Costs can vary based on the complexity of your case, the dentist's experience, and your insurance coverage.

What to Expect

The typical traditional braces procedure in Lubbock takes about 18-24 months. Most dental offices in the area accept major insurance plans, and many offer financing options for procedures not fully covered by insurance.

How to Choose a Provider

When selecting a dentist for traditional braces in Lubbock, consider their experience, patient reviews, and whether they offer consultations. Board-certified specialists often provide the best outcomes for complex procedures.
