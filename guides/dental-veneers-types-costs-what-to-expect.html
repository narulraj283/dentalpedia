Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental veneers cost?

Porcelain veneers cost $800-$2,000 per tooth, while composite veneers range from $250-$1,500 per tooth.

How long do veneers last?

Porcelain veneers typically last 10-15 years, while composite veneers last 5-7 years before replacement.

Are veneers reversible?

Veneers require permanent tooth preparation, making them essentially irreversible. This is an important consideration.

Do veneers look natural?

Professional porcelain veneers look very natural and are custom-made to match your other teeth.

How do I care for veneers?

Maintain normal brushing and flossing, avoid hard foods, don't use teeth as tools, and visit your dentist regularly.
