Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if a tooth is knocked out?

Find the tooth, rinse it gently without scrubbing, try to reinsert it or keep it in milk, and get to a dentist within 30 minutes.

Is a severe toothache an emergency?

Severe pain with swelling or fever indicates infection and should be treated immediately.

What should I do for a broken crown?

Contact your dentist for urgent appointment. Temporarily, avoid chewing on that side and use over-the-counter pain relief.

Can I go to an ER for dental emergencies?

ERs can provide pain management and antibiotics for infections but not definitive dental treatment. See an emergency dentist if possible.

How much does emergency dental care cost?

Emergency visits typically cost $150-$500, with treatment costs varying based on the specific issue.
