Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dental fillings last?

Composite fillings last 5-10 years, while amalgam fillings last 10-15 years on average.

Are amalgam fillings safe?

Amalgam is deemed safe by health organizations despite mercury content. Many people prefer composite for aesthetic reasons.

How much do dental fillings cost?

Composite fillings cost $150-$300 per tooth, while amalgam fillings are slightly less expensive at $100-$200.

Is the filling procedure painful?

The area is numbed with anesthesia, so the procedure is painless. Sensitivity may occur briefly after.

Why do I need a filling?

Fillings treat cavities caused by decay, preventing further damage and restoring tooth function.
