Frequently Asked Questions

Are root canals painful?

Root canals use local anesthesia to numb the tooth. Most people experience minimal pain. Discomfort after treatment is manageable with medication.

How much does a root canal cost?

Root canals typically cost $700-$1,500 per tooth. Front teeth are usually less expensive than molars.

How long does a root canal take?

Most root canals take 30-90 minutes, depending on the tooth's complexity and number of roots.

What happens after root canal treatment?

You'll need a crown or restoration to protect the tooth. Avoid chewing on that side until the permanent restoration is completed.

Can a root canal fail?

About 90-95% of root canals are successful. Retreatment can be performed if the canal becomes infected again.
