Frequently Asked Questions

Is cosmetic dentistry covered by insurance?

Most cosmetic procedures are not covered by insurance. Some procedures like crowns may be covered if they're medically necessary.

How do I choose cosmetic procedures?

Consult with your dentist about your goals, and discuss which procedures would benefit your smile most.

What is a smile design consultation?

A consultation where your dentist evaluates your smile and discusses procedures to achieve your desired aesthetic goals.

How long do cosmetic results last?

Results vary by procedure, ranging from 6 months for whitening to 15+ years for veneers and crowns.

Can cosmetic dentistry improve my health?

While primarily cosmetic, these procedures can improve oral function and may boost confidence and psychological well-being.
