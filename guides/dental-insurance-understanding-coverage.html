Frequently Asked Questions

What does dental insurance typically cover?

Most plans cover preventive care (100%), basic procedures (70-80%), and major services (50%) with deductibles and annual maximums.

How much does dental insurance cost?

Premiums range from $100-$400 per month depending on plan type and coverage level.

What is a deductible in dental insurance?

A deductible is the amount you pay out-of-pocket before insurance begins covering services, typically $25-$100 per year.

What is an annual maximum?

The maximum amount insurance will pay per year, typically $1,000-$2,000, after which you pay 100%.

Should I buy dental insurance or use discount plans?

Insurance is better for planned extensive work. Discount plans work well for preventive care and unexpected emergencies.
