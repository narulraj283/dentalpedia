Frequently Asked Questions

What foods are best for dental health?

Calcium-rich dairy, crunchy fruits and vegetables, fatty fish, nuts, and water promote strong teeth and gums.

How does sugar damage teeth?

Sugar feeds cavity-causing bacteria, which produce acids that damage tooth enamel. Frequent consumption increases risk.

Are acidic foods bad for teeth?

Acidic foods and drinks like citrus, soda, and wine erode enamel. Consume in moderation and rinse with water after.

What drinks are best for dental health?

Water is ideal. Fluoridated water strengthens teeth. Avoid sugary drinks, soda, and excessive alcohol.

Does cheese really protect teeth?

Yes, cheese raises mouth pH and contains calcium, helping neutralize acids and strengthen teeth.
