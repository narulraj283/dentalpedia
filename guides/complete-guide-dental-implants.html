Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental implants cost?

Dental implants typically range from $1,500-$6,000 per tooth, with total mouth restoration costing $20,000-$100,000. Cost varies by location and complexity.

Are dental implants painful?

The procedure uses anesthesia so you won't feel pain during surgery. Post-operative discomfort is usually manageable with prescribed pain medication.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, dental implants can last 25-30 years or even a lifetime. The crown may need replacement after 10-15 years.

Who is a good candidate for dental implants?

You need good oral health, adequate jawbone density, and commitment to oral hygiene. Age is not a limiting factor if you're healthy.

How long does the implant process take?

From start to finish, the process typically takes 3-9 months, depending on bone grafting needs and healing time.
