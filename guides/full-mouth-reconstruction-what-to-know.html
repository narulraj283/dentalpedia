Frequently Asked Questions

How much does full mouth reconstruction cost?

Full mouth reconstruction typically costs $20,000-$100,000+ depending on the extent of treatment and materials used.

How long does full mouth reconstruction take?

The process takes 6-24 months depending on complexity, bone grafting needs, and number of procedures required.

Why would someone need full mouth reconstruction?

Reasons include severe decay, tooth loss, gum disease, bite problems, or worn dentition requiring comprehensive treatment.

Is full mouth reconstruction covered by insurance?

Coverage varies. Some procedures may be covered if deemed medically necessary, while cosmetic portions typically aren't.

What is the first step in reconstruction?

A comprehensive evaluation including X-rays, 3D imaging, bite analysis, and treatment planning by the dentist.
