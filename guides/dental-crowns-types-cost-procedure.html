Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dental crown cost?

Crowns typically cost $800-$1,500 per tooth, varying by material and location.

How long does a crown last?

Dental crowns typically last 10-15 years, sometimes longer with proper care.

Are crowns painful?

The procedure uses anesthesia so it's painless. Slight sensitivity after placement is normal and usually resolves quickly.

What is the difference between porcelain and metal crowns?

Porcelain crowns look natural but are less durable. Metal crowns are stronger but less aesthetic.

Can a crown fail?

Crowns can crack, chip, or decay at the margins. Regular dental visits help catch problems early.
