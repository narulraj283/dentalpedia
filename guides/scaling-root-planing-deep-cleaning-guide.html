Frequently Asked Questions

How much does scaling and root planing cost?

SRP typically costs $400-$1,200 depending on how many areas of the mouth need treatment.

Is scaling and root planing painful?

The procedure may be uncomfortable, but local anesthesia minimizes pain. Numbness prevents pain during treatment.

How long does scaling and root planing take?

The procedure typically takes 30-60 minutes per quadrant and may require multiple appointments.

How effective is scaling and root planing?

SRP effectively treats gum disease in about 90% of cases when combined with improved oral hygiene.

What should I expect after treatment?

Some bleeding and sensitivity is normal. Your gums should improve over 2-4 weeks with proper care.
