Frequently Asked Questions

Is tooth loss inevitable with age?

No, tooth loss is not inevitable. With proper care and preventive measures, natural teeth can be maintained throughout life.

Why do seniors experience more dry mouth?

Dry mouth is caused by age-related changes in saliva production and as a side effect of many medications.

How does dry mouth affect oral health?

Dry mouth increases cavity and infection risk. Saliva is essential for protecting teeth and fighting bacteria.

What dental problems are common in seniors?

Common issues include cavities, gum disease, tooth loss, oral cancer, root decay, and medication-related problems.

How often should seniors visit the dentist?

Seniors should visit at least twice yearly, more frequently if they have gum disease or other conditions.
