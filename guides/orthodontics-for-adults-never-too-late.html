Frequently Asked Questions

Is it too late to get braces as an adult?

No, orthodontics works at any age. Healthy bone and gums are more important than age.

How long does adult orthodontic treatment take?

Adult treatment typically takes 18-36 months, sometimes longer than for teenagers due to bone density.

Are clear aligners good for adults?

Yes, aligners are popular with adults for their discrete appearance and convenience.

How much do adult braces cost?

Adult orthodontics typically costs $3,000-$8,000 depending on treatment type and duration.

Can orthodontics help with bite problems in adults?

Yes, orthodontics can correct bite problems. Severe cases may require combined orthodontic and surgical treatment.
