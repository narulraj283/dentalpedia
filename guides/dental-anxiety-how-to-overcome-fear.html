Frequently Asked Questions

Is dental anxiety common?

Yes, about 15% of people have significant dental anxiety, and 50% experience some level of fear.

What triggers dental anxiety?

Common triggers include fear of pain, loss of control, medical trauma, and past negative experiences.

Can sedation dentistry help?

Yes, options range from nitrous oxide to IV sedation, allowing fearful patients to receive needed care.

How do I find a dentist who works with anxious patients?

Look for dentists experienced in sedation dentistry and patient testimonials about their calming approach.

What can I do before my appointment to reduce anxiety?

Relaxation techniques, visualization, arriving early, and discussing concerns with staff can help reduce fear.
