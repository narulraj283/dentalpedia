Frequently Asked Questions

Why do wisdom teeth need to be removed?

Wisdom teeth are often removed due to crowding, impaction, decay, or infection. Many don't have enough space in the jaw.

How much does wisdom teeth removal cost?

Simple extractions cost $200-$400 per tooth. Impacted teeth may cost $600-$2,000 per tooth.

What is the recovery time?

Most people recover in 7-10 days, with complete healing taking 2-3 weeks. Swelling peaks on days 2-3.

Is wisdom teeth removal painful?

The procedure uses anesthesia for pain-free extraction. Post-operative soreness is managed with medication and ice.

Should all wisdom teeth be removed?

No. If wisdom teeth are healthy, properly positioned, and cleanable, they may not need removal.
