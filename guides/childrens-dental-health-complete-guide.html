Frequently Asked Questions

When should a child's first dental visit be?

The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends the first visit by age 1 or when the first tooth appears.

How often should children see the dentist?

Children should visit the dentist every 6 months for checkups and cleanings.

When do baby teeth fall out?

Baby teeth typically start shedding around age 6 and are mostly gone by age 12.

What should I do about baby bottle tooth decay?

Avoid putting babies to sleep with bottles of milk, juice, or formula. Clean gums daily and introduce water after meals.

Do children need fluoride?

Yes, fluoride helps prevent cavities. Dentists often apply fluoride treatments or recommend fluoride toothpaste.
