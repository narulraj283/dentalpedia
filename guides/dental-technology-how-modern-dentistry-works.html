Frequently Asked Questions

Are digital X-rays safer than traditional X-rays?

Yes, digital X-rays expose you to significantly less radiation than traditional film X-rays.

What is CAD/CAM technology in dentistry?

Computer-aided design and manufacturing allows dentists to design and create crowns, veneers, and restorations in a single visit.

What are the benefits of laser dentistry?

Lasers provide more precise treatment, reduced bleeding, faster healing, and often eliminate the need for anesthesia.

How is 3D printing used in dentistry?

3D printing creates custom implant guides, crowns, dentures, orthodontic models, and surgical tools.

Can I have a dental appointment online?

Tele-dentistry consultations are available for many situations, though in-person visits are needed for most treatments.
