Frequently Asked Questions

What causes TMJ disorder?

Causes include jaw injury, teeth grinding, stress, arthritis, misalignment, and poor posture.

What are the symptoms of TMJ disorder?

Symptoms include jaw pain, clicking or popping sounds, difficulty opening the mouth, headaches, and ear pain.

How is TMJ disorder diagnosed?

Diagnosis involves physical examination, imaging (MRI or CT scan), and history assessment. Sometimes arthroscopy is needed.

Can TMJ disorder be cured?

Most cases improve with conservative treatment. Some may require ongoing management or surgery.

What home remedies help TMJ pain?

Ice packs, heat therapy, jaw exercises, stress reduction, and avoiding hard foods can help manage symptoms.
