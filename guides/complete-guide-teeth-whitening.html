Frequently Asked Questions

How much does professional teeth whitening cost?

Professional in-office whitening typically costs $300-$1,000, while take-home trays range from $150-$500.

How long do teeth whitening results last?

Results typically last 6-12 months with proper maintenance. Touch-ups can extend the brightness.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Professional whitening is safe when done by a dentist. Over-the-counter products should be used as directed to avoid sensitivity.

Can whitening damage my teeth?

Professional whitening is enamel-safe. Overuse of whitening products can cause sensitivity and enamel erosion.

Does whitening work on crowns or veneers?

Whitening doesn't affect crowns or veneers. You may need to replace them to match newly whitened natural teeth.
