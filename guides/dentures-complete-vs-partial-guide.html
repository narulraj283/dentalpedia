Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dentures cost?

Complete dentures range from $1,000-$3,000, while partial dentures cost $500-$2,000 depending on material and design.

How long does it take to get dentures?

The complete process takes 3-8 weeks, including impressions, fittings, and adjustments.

Do dentures feel natural?

Modern dentures are more comfortable than ever, but most people need an adjustment period to feel natural wearing them.

How should I clean my dentures?

Rinse after eating, brush daily with a denture brush, soak overnight in denture solution, and handle carefully to avoid breaking.

How often do dentures need replacement?

Dentures typically last 5-7 years. Your jaw changes over time, requiring refitting or replacement.
