Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper: braces or Invisalign?

Traditional braces typically cost $3,000-$7,000, while Invisalign ranges from $3,500-$8,000. Costs vary by location and complexity.

Is Invisalign as effective as braces?

Invisalign works well for mild to moderate misalignment, but braces are more effective for severe cases or complex bite issues.

How long do braces take to work?

Braces typically require 18-24 months of treatment, depending on the severity of misalignment.

Can you eat normally with Invisalign?

Yes, you remove Invisalign trays to eat. With braces, you need to avoid hard, sticky, and crunchy foods.

Are Invisalign aligners noticeable?

Invisalign trays are nearly invisible and much less noticeable than traditional braces, which is a major advantage.
