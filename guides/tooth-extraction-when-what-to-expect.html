Frequently Asked Questions

How much does tooth extraction cost?

Simple extractions cost $200-$400 per tooth. Surgical extractions may cost $600-$2,000 depending on complexity.

Is tooth extraction painful?

Local anesthesia ensures the procedure is painless. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with medication.

What should I do after tooth extraction?

Rest, apply ice, avoid straw use, don't rinse or spit, avoid smoking, and follow your dentist's specific aftercare instructions.

What complications can occur after extraction?

Possible complications include dry socket, infection, excessive bleeding, and nerve damage, though these are rare.

When can I eat after extraction?

Start with soft foods after the anesthesia wears off. Avoid hard, sticky, or hot foods for the first week.
