Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best mouthguard?

Custom-made mouthguards offer superior protection and comfort compared to over-the-counter options.

How much do custom mouthguards cost?

Custom mouthguards typically cost $200-$500, but offer better protection than cheaper alternatives.

What should I do if a tooth is knocked out?

Find the tooth, rinse gently, try to reinsert it, or keep it in milk, and seek emergency dental care immediately.

Can chipped teeth be repaired?

Yes, chips can be repaired with bonding or crowns depending on severity.

Which sports have the highest dental injury risk?

Contact sports like football, hockey, and rugby have high injury rates. Basketball and baseball also pose risks.
