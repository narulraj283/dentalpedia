Frequently Asked Questions

Is mercury in amalgam fillings dangerous?

Health organizations consider amalgam safe, but holistic dentists prefer alternatives like composite or ceramic materials.

What are biocompatible materials?

Biocompatible materials are dental materials that don't trigger immune or allergic reactions and are compatible with the body.

What natural products help oral health?

Oil pulling, herbal rinses, and certain foods can support oral health, but scientific evidence is limited.

Can holistic dentistry prevent all dental disease?

While prevention is emphasized, some conditions still require professional dental treatment regardless of approach.

Is holistic dentistry more expensive?

Holistic dentistry using premium materials can be more expensive, though costs vary by practitioner and materials used.
