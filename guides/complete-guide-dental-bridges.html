Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dental bridge cost?

Dental bridges typically cost $1,500-$5,000 depending on the type and materials used.

How long do dental bridges last?

Bridges usually last 5-15 years. Proper care and oral hygiene can extend their lifespan.

What is the difference between bridge types?

Traditional bridges use adjacent teeth for support, cantilever bridges use one support tooth, and resin-bonded bridges use minimal tooth preparation.

How do I clean under a bridge?

Use floss threaders or water flossers to clean under the bridge. Brush normally and avoid chewing on hard foods.

Are bridges as good as implants?

Implants are superior long-term, but bridges are suitable for many patients and require less treatment time.
