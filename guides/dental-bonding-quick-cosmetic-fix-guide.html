Frequently Asked Questions

How much does dental bonding cost?

Dental bonding typically costs $250-$600 per tooth, making it an affordable cosmetic option.

How long does dental bonding last?

Bonding typically lasts 5-10 years, requiring periodic maintenance and touch-ups.

Is dental bonding permanent?

No, bonding is not permanent and will eventually need replacement as the composite material wears over time.

What can dental bonding fix?

Bonding can repair chips, cracks, gaps between teeth, misaligned teeth, and discoloration.

Is bonding as durable as veneers?

No, bonding is less durable than porcelain veneers but is more affordable and requires less tooth preparation.
