Dental Implants
"Immediate Load Implants: Prosthetic Restoration on Implant Placement Day"
Immediate load implant protocols deliver functional prosthetic restoration on implant placement day, eliminating the 3-6 month healing wait of traditional osseointegration. Stringent primary stability requirements (>35 Ncm torque, ISQ >65), occlusal design modifications, and careful patient selection optimize success while maintaining implant survival rates equivalent to conventional approaches.
