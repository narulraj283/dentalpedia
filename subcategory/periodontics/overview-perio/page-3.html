Red Complex Bacteria and Most Virulent Periodontal Pathogens
Socransky's red complex bacteria—particularly P. gingivalis, T. denticola, and T. forsythia—represent the most virulent periodontal pathogens, utilizing sophisticated virulence mechanisms and synergistic interactions that drive severe periodontitis.

Cost of Gum Disease Stages
Gingivitis treatment costs $200-400 annually while moderate periodontitis averages $1,500-3,000 and severe disease exceeds $5,000-15,000 in annual management.
