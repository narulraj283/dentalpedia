Editorial Standards

How DentalPedia ensures quality, accuracy, and trustworthiness

Our Commitment to Quality

DentalPedia is committed to providing accurate, evidence-based dental health information. Our editorial board consists of board-certified dental professionals who review all content to ensure clinical accuracy and relevance.

Editorial Review Process

Every article published on DentalPedia undergoes a rigorous review process:

  • Clinical Accuracy: All content is reviewed by board-certified specialists in the relevant dental field
  • Evidence-Based Information: Claims are supported by peer-reviewed research and clinical guidelines
  • Plain Language: Complex dental concepts are explained in accessible language for patients
  • Regular Updates: Articles are periodically reviewed and updated to reflect current best practices

Editorial Board

Our editorial board includes board-certified dentists specializing in:

  • General Dentistry
  • Prosthodontics
  • Orthodontics
  • Periodontics
  • Endodontics
  • Oral Surgery
  • Pediatric Dentistry
  • Cosmetic Dentistry

Information Sources

DentalPedia articles reference authoritative sources including:

  • American Dental Association (ADA)
  • National Institutes of Health (NIH)
  • PubMed Central
  • Peer-reviewed dental journals
  • Evidence-based clinical guidelines

Medical Disclaimer

The information provided on DentalPedia is for educational purposes only and should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with a qualified dentist before making any treatment decisions or changing your dental care routine.

Accuracy & Updates

While we strive for accuracy, dental science evolves continuously. If you notice any inaccuracies or have concerns about our content, please contact us with specific details.
