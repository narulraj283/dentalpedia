About DentalPedia

Our Mission

DentalPedia is a free, open dental encyclopedia that combines the speed and scale of artificial intelligence with the expertise of licensed dental professionals. Our goal is to make accurate, comprehensive dental health information accessible to everyone.

Every article on DentalPedia is AI-generated and then reviewed by a licensed dentist or dental specialist. We believe this hybrid approach — combining AI's ability to synthesize vast amounts of medical literature with human clinical expertise — produces the most accurate and up-to-date dental reference available.

How It Works

Our content pipeline works in three stages. First, our AI system generates draft articles by synthesizing information from peer-reviewed dental journals, clinical guidelines from the ADA and other professional organizations, and established dental textbooks. Second, each article is reviewed by a licensed dental professional who verifies accuracy, adds clinical nuance, and flags any issues. Third, articles are published with full transparency — each page shows its AI-generated status, its expert reviewer, and its source citations.

The site is updated weekly with new articles and revisions to existing content as dental science evolves.

For Dental Professionals

We actively seek partnerships with licensed dentists and dental specialists to serve as expert reviewers. In exchange for reviewing articles in your area of expertise, your name, credentials, and practice website are prominently linked on every article you review — providing valuable E-E-A-T signals for your own website's SEO while helping patients access trustworthy information.

If you're a licensed dental professional interested in contributing, please get in touch.

Editorial Standards

DentalPedia adheres to the following editorial principles:

  • Every article cites its sources, linking to peer-reviewed research, ADA guidelines, or established clinical references.
  • Every article is reviewed by a licensed dental professional whose credentials are displayed on the page.
  • We are transparent that our content is AI-generated and clearly label this on every page.
  • We do not provide personalized medical advice. All content is informational and encourages readers to consult their own dentist.
  • We update articles as new evidence and guidelines emerge.
